Potica

Description

Potica is a traditional festive pastry from Slovenia.

Potica consists of a rolled pastry made of leavened paper-thin dough filled with any of a great variety of fillings, but most often with walnut filling.

Ingreedients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Egg yolk
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Walnuts
  • Honey
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Add ingreedients.
  3. Sprinkle the dough with flour.
  4. Roll out the dough.
  5. Add filling.
  6. Roll it tightly.
  7. Bake, bake, bake.
